My Results

What do the results of these tests mean for you?

Showed me my strengths and weaknesses, with how I learn and work. They've identified areas where I can work on improvement.

How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?

I will work to my strengths, and work on my weaknesses. I will communicate to the group to ensure there is understanding, from where I am coming from, and where the rest of the group is placed.

How should you take this into account when forming a team?

As they identified strengths and weaknesses, I can select other members who excel in areas I struggle with, this will give the team overall balance. We can also target work at those with the strongest attributes.
